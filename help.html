Frequently Asked Questions

To place an order, browse our menu, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your order.

Once an order is placed, modifications are not possible. However, you can cancel the order within a short period if it hasn't been processed.

We accept credit/debit cards, online wallets, and cash on delivery depending on your location.

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can track the delivery status in real time.

If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our support team immediately at support@foodify.com, and we'll assist you promptly.
